Ayn Rand


Ayn Rand was a bestselling author and social critic who wrote novels, plays, and philosophy books.

Her philosophy, which she described as Objectivism, holds that any proper moral system must use objective reality as its starting point. Rand believed that humans are beings with certain natural needs and wants, and that any action to fulfill those needs and wants is perfectly just and moral so long as each individual never initiates the use of force against a fellow human.

Rand's writings have been an inspiration for countless people around the globe, from business tycoons to libertarian activists.
